Overview

Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is the student body of the Computer Engineering undergraduates of University of Peradeniya. Every year ACES organizes various events and activities with the aid of the highly qualified academic staff members to enlighten the knowledge of the undergraduates. This time ACES Spark focuses on Machine Learning webinar series and aims students to deep dive into the data driven world with real impact. At the end of each webinar session, students will be given a keggle challenge to compete in order to inspire students to have hands-on experiences. .
