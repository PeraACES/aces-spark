Spark

Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) is the student body of the Computer Engineering undergraduates of Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya. Every year ACES organizes various events & activities with the aid of highly qualified academic staff members to enlighten the knowledge of the undergraduates and to serve the society. Inter School Quiz Competition is one of the events which is organized annually to benefit Advanced Level students.

Our Objectives

Project Background

With the evolvement of Information Technology, youth should acquire high level of knowledge for the development of the nation & to compete with the world. A/L students of Mathematical stream are targeted for this project because they are capable of capturing the knowledge of new technology with the basic knowledge they already have. To initiate this project, required assistance & the guidance will be given by the highly qualified academic staff members & the alumni of ACES. The feedback of the participants of previous years have proven the success of this programme.

Objectives

  • To develop & assess the IT knowledge of A/L students
  • To provide the experience by working with engineering students
  • To witness the role of the computer engineer
  • To adapt to working environment & following a time frame
  • To transmit the knowledge to the society via the participants
